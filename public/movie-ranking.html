The Best Movies According to Apoorv

My top 5 movies & movie series of all time.

The Karate Kid Series

This movie series give life lessons beautifully through the use of Karate.

Deadpool & Wolverine

My favourite comic book characters Deadpool & Wolverine have been fantastically portrayed by Ryan Reynolds and Hugh Jackman respectively. The movie is full of humour, emotion and action.

MCU's Doctor Strange

The portrayal of Doctor Strange by Benedict Cumberbatch is superb. Really awesome movies.

The Dark Knight Trilogy

Christopher Nolan's direction is superb & Christian Bale's performance as Batman is brilliant. This movie series is at the top spot in superhero genre according to me.

Spider-Man's Trilogy

Sam Raimi's trilogy is captivating. Tobey Maguire is amazing as Spider-Man.
